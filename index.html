Message from Me

Hello and welcome my portfolio! I am Lee White, also known as LeekTree in online spaces.

I am a graphic designer that dabbles in web design and other forms of art. I graduated from Lake Land College in 2024 with a Graphic Design degree, a Web Design Certificate, a Desktop Publishing Certificate, and a Computer Application Specialist Certificate. This portfolio is part work, part fun, and all me. Everything you'll see was earned or made by me, including this website!
